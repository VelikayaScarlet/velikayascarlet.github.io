# Poetry

A small collection of classical Chinese verses I've found moving, or attempted to write. (Edit or remove this paragraph as you like.)

<div class="poem">
<span class="poem__title">静夜思</span>
<span class="poem__meta">李白 · 唐代</span>
床前明月光，
疑是地上霜。
举头望明月，
低头思故乡。
</div>

<div class="poem">
<span class="poem__title">春晓</span>
<span class="poem__meta">孟浩然 · 唐代</span>
春眠不觉晓，
处处闻啼鸟。
夜来风雨声，
花落知多少。
</div>
